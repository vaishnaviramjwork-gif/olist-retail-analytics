# 🛒 Olist E-Commerce Analytics — End-to-End Retail Analysis

![Dashboard](Olist%20E-Commerce%20Analytics%20Dashboard.png)

## 📌 Project Overview
End-to-end business analysis of 100,000+ orders from Olist, Brazil's largest e-commerce platform. This project covers customer segmentation, revenue trend analysis, delivery performance and product category insights — built entirely using SQL logic, Python, Excel and Tableau.

**Tools Used:** Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn) · Excel · Tableau · Jupyter Notebook

**Dataset:** [Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce)

## 🔍 Analysis Breakdown

### 1️⃣ Revenue Trend Analysis
- Extracted monthly revenue from 100k+ orders
- Identified Black Friday 2017 as peak revenue month
- Revenue grew 8x from Jan 2017 to Nov 2017

### 2️⃣ RFM Customer Segmentation
Segmented 93,000+ unique customers into 6 groups:
- **Champions** — 6,459 customers (highest value)
- **Loyal Customers** — 14,213 customers
- **New Customers** — 14,980 customers
- **Potential Loyalists** — 38,189 customers (biggest opportunity)
- **At Risk** — 13,226 customers (need win-back campaign)
- **Lost Customers** — 6,283 customers

### 3️⃣ Delivery Performance
- Analysed delivery times across all 27 Brazilian states
- São Paulo (SP) fastest — avg 8 days
- Roraima (RR) slowest — avg 28 days
- Identified states needing logistics investment

### 4️⃣ Product Category Analysis
- Ranked top 10 categories by revenue
- Analysed review scores by category
- Identified lowest rated categories for operations team focus

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. **Win-back campaign** for 13,226 At Risk customers — they spent R$318 on average and haven't bought in 393 days
2. **Loyalty programme** for 38,189 Potential Loyalists — largest segment, high conversion opportunity
3. **Logistics review** for northern states — RR, AP, AM averaging 25+ days delivery vs SP's 8 days
4. **Category focus** — invest in worst rated product categories to improve review scores and reduce returns
